Stages of rational decision-making process:
1. Define the problem (the goal)
2. Identify the criteria to evaluate the problem (to achieve the goal)
3. Weight the criteria in terms of importance to you
4. Generate alternatives or identify possible course of action
5. Assess each alternative based on each criteria 
6. Calculate optimal

Example:
Goal: Apply for an internship

| Criteria   | Criterion weight | Option 1   | Value Opt 1 (1-3) | Option 2    | Value Opt 2 (1-3) |
| ---------- | ---------------- | ---------- | ----------------- | ----------- | ----------------- |
| Distance   | 1                | EPAM 4.7km | 3                 | Yandex 10km | 2                 |
| Complexity | 3                | Medium     | 2                 | Hard        | 1                 |
| Salary     | 2                | free       | 0                 | 300$        | 3                 |

* Option 1: $$1*3+3*2+2*0=9$$
* Option 2: $$1*2+3*1+2*3=11$$  
The choice will be option 2
